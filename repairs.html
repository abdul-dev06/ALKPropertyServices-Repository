Why It Matters Here

Calgary's Climate
Is Hard on Homes

Extreme Cold Breaks Things

At -30°C, materials contract, seals crack, and pipes freeze. Calgary winters put more stress on a home than most Canadian cities. Caulking around windows and doors deteriorates faster here, letting in cold air and moisture damage.

Chinook Winds Stress Structures

Calgary's famous Chinook winds create rapid pressure changes that stress fencing, gates, and doors. What works fine in summer starts sticking, warping, or breaking by spring. Regular maintenance keeps these issues small and affordable.

Deferred Repairs Cost More

A small leak ignored becomes water damage. A sticking door becomes a broken frame. In Calgary's climate, problems compound quickly. Catching and fixing issues early is always cheaper than dealing with them after the damage spreads.
