Why It Matters Here

Calgary Homes Need
Quality Drywall

🌡️

Chinook Freeze-Thaw Cycles

Calgary's dramatic temperature swings — sometimes 30°C in a single day — cause homes to shift and settle. This puts constant stress on drywall, leading to cracks at seams, corners, and around openings that need professional attention to fix properly.

💧

Winter Pipe Bursts & Flooding

At -30°C, pipes freeze and burst regularly in Calgary homes. When they do, the drywall absorbs the damage. Flood recovery drywall isn't just about looks — it's about removing moisture properly to prevent mould growth behind the walls.

🏗️

Growing City, New Construction

Calgary continues to grow rapidly, with countless basement developments, garage conversions, and home additions underway. Quality drywall work is essential for getting a proper finish on new spaces and meeting resale standards in a competitive market.

How We Work

Our Drywall Process

Every job follows the same careful steps — no skipping coats, no rushing dry time.

01

Assessment

We assess the space, take measurements, and walk you through what needs to be done and why.

02

Boarding

Drywall sheets are cut, fitted, and fastened securely to studs with proper spacing and screws.

03

Taping

Seams and corners are taped and given a first coat of mud to begin building a smooth surface.

04

Finishing Coats

Multiple finishing coats are applied and allowed to fully dry between each pass — no shortcuts.

05

Sanding

The surface is sanded smooth, ready for primer and paint with no visible seams or ridges.
