About ALK Property Services

Local. Honest.
No Runaround.

We're a locally owned property services company based in Calgary. We focus on drywall, home repairs, and painting.

Every job gets a straight quote before we start, the right materials, and the time it needs to be done properly. We're not there to hurry, we're there to finish it right.

We Stand Behind Our Work

If something isn't right after we've finished, we come back and fix it. No chasing, no excuses.

Priced Upfront, No Surprises

We give you a clear number before work starts. If something occurs, or plans change, we clearly communicate with you before continuing. That's what's on the invoice when we're done — nothing added at the end.

We Show Up When We Say

Scheduled time is kept. If something changes on our end, you hear from us in advance — not the morning of.
