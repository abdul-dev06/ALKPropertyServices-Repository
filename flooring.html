What Sets Us Apart

The Details Matter

Subfloor First

We assess and prep the subfloor before laying anything. Uneven, squeaky, or damaged subfloors are addressed — not ignored.

Tight, Clean Seams

Proper acclimation, careful cuts, and patient layout produce floors that look as good at the walls as they do in the middle of the room.

We Clean Up

Old flooring, offcuts, and packaging leave with us. You get a finished floor, not a construction site.

The Process

From Quote to Done

01

Measure & Estimate

We come out, measure the space, and check the subfloor condition. You'll get a clear quote that includes removal, prep, and installation.

02

Material Selection

If you haven't chosen a product yet, we'll recommend options in your budget. We supply material at fair pricing or we work with what you've bought.

03

Prep & Install

We remove old flooring, address the subfloor as needed, and install your new floor according to manufacturer specs.

04

Finishing Touches

Baseboards and transitions go back in, the space is cleaned up, and we walk through the finished job together before we leave.
